AIRPORT GUIDE
Step by step.
Tap each stage to see what to expect.
1
Check-in
2–3 hrs before departure
▼
🎫
Online check-in opens 24–48h before — do it from your phone to pick your seat early.
🧳
Bag drop is separate from check-in — follow airport signs. Have your booking reference ready.
⚖️
Bag weight is checked at drop-off. Overweight fees can be A$20–$50 per kg — weigh at home first.
2
Baggage drop
After check-in
▼
🏷️
Bag tag is printed at check-in — attach it before joining the drop queue.
🔒
Lock your bag with a TSA-approved lock — security can open it without breaking the lock.
3
Security screening
Allow 20–30 min
▼
💻
Laptop and large electronics go in a separate tray — remove before the belt.
💧
Liquids rule — max 100ml per container in one clear zip-lock bag. Buy water after security.
👟
Shoes and belt — some airports ask you to remove these. Watch what people ahead of you do.
4
Departure immigration
BNE — SmartGate available
▼
🛂
SmartGate at BNE — use the automated lane if you have an e-passport. Much faster.
📄
Have ready — passport, boarding pass, and student visa grant letter accessible on your phone.
😌
Stay calm — questions are routine. See Checklist tab for what you may be asked.
5
Transit / layover
At your connecting airport
▼
🔄
Connection time — under 90 min is tight for international transfers. Ask staff if unsure.
🌿
Changi (SIN) 4h+ layover? Butterfly garden, Jewel waterfall, free shower — all airside.
🍜
Eat at the transit airport — often better and cheaper than on the plane.
6
Boarding
Gate closes 20–30 min early
▼
🚪
Gate closes early — be at the gate 45 min before departure time, not at departure.
🎒
Overhead space fills fast — board early if you have a big carry-on bag.
📵
Flight mode once doors close. Download movies and offline maps before boarding.
7
Arrival & customs
At your home country
▼
🛬
Immigration first — follow signs to passport control before baggage claim.
🧳
Baggage claim — check screens for your flight number and belt. Allow 20–40 min.
📝
Customs declaration — declare electronics, cash over limit, and any food or plant items.
🚗
Onward journey — pre-book your ride or arrange pickup before you land.
