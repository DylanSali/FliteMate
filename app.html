Airport Guide
Step by step.
Tap each stage to see what to expect.
1
Check-in
2–3 hrs before departure
▼
🎫
Online check-in opens 24–48h before — do it from your phone to pick your seat early.
🧳
Bag drop is separate — follow airport signs. Have your booking reference ready.
⚖️
Bag weight is checked at drop-off. Overweight fees: A$20–50 per kg — weigh at home first.
2
Baggage drop
After check-in
▼
🏷️
Bag tag is printed at check-in — attach it before joining the drop queue.
🔒
TSA-approved lock — security can open it without breaking it.
3
Security screening
Allow 20–30 min
▼
💻
Laptop and large electronics go in a separate tray — remove before the belt.
💧
Liquids rule — max 100ml per container in one clear zip-lock bag.
👟
Shoes and belt — some airports ask you to remove these.
4
Departure immigration
BNE — SmartGate available
▼
🛂
SmartGate at BNE — use the automated lane if you have an e-passport.
📄
Have ready — passport, boarding pass, and student visa grant letter.
😌
Stay calm — questions are routine. See Checklist for full list.
5
Transit / layover
At your connecting airport
▼
🔄
Under 90 min is tight for international connections — ask staff if unsure.
🌿
Changi SIN — 4h+ layover? Butterfly garden, Jewel waterfall, free shower — all airside.
6
Boarding
Gate closes 20–30 min early
▼
🚪
Gate closes early — be there 45 min before departure, not at departure time.
📵
Flight mode once doors close. Download movies before boarding.
7
Arrival & customs
At your home country
▼
🛬
Immigration first — then baggage claim — then customs declaration.
🧳
Baggage claim — check screens for your flight and belt. Allow 20–40 min.
📝
Customs — declare electronics, cash over limit, any food or plant items.
